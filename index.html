The ACTIV group

Offical website: ACTIV group

The Advanced CT Imaging and Visualization (ACTIV) group is a research group at the University Medical Center Freiburg. We are part of the Department of Radiology - Medical Physics and the Advanced Molecular Imaging Research Center (AMIR). Our research focuses on the development of advanced imaging techniques in computed tomography.
